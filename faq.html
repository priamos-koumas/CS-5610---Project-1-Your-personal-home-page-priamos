Q&A

Some of my hobbies and interests outside of technology include hanging out with friends, going to the movies, going to the gym, watching my favorite sports like basketball, soccer, and football, and lastly traveling to Greece to visit my family.

I am fluent in both English and Greek.

I grew up in Fall River, Massachusetts, which is a city located in the southeastern part of the state, about an hour drive away from Boston.

My father's side of the family is from Maroussi, Athens, Greece, and my mother's side of the family is from a small village called Pyrgi which is located on the island of Chios in Greece.

My parents initially moved to Montreal, Canada to pursue better academic opportunities. After a few years, the opportunity arose for them to become professors at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth, so they moved to the United States and have been living in Massachusetts ever since, making my siblings and me first-generation Americans.

My favorite Greek instrument to listen to is certainly the bouzouki.
